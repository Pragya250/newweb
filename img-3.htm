    Zomato Pro Membership

    Zomato Pro membership helps individuals find the best deals and get an overall better service compared to normal users. The Zomato Pro subscription is an upgrade from the previous Zomato Gold programme which used to exist. Albeit providing the same benefits, there have been certain changes with the newly updated subscription such as a Zomato Pro free delivery. Read further to know more about the Zomato Pro benefits.

    Zomato Pro Membership Benefits

    Zomato Pro features exclusive services for members who have subscribed to the food delivery giant. You will find features that make ordering food a fun and enjoyable occasion with Zomato. With priority over normal customers, make sure to use the membership to its full potential. The benefits of Zomato Pro are mentioned below.

    • You get additional discounts on orders even after applying existing discount offers.
    • Get 20% faster delivery so that wait times for your delicious meal is significantly reduced.
    • Receive up to 40% off on total bill value when dining.
    • Up to 30% extra off on delivery using Zomato Pro.
    • There are no limits as to when you can use the benefit. Be it every day, weekly or monthly, you are not subject to a specified number of uses.
    • Additional discounts with online orders.
    • Faster delivery with more preference.
    • Discounts on the dine-in bills.

    Zomato Pro Terms And Conditions

    As with any offer or special benefit provided by companies for users, there will be certain rules and regulations the user must adhere to in order to acquire smooth functioning of the provided features without any cancellation. As such, Zomato Pro has certain terms and conditions that need to be followed when using the Pro subscription which is mentioned below.

    • There are no daily, weekly or monthly limits when using the Pro feature.
    • The membership can only be used on a single account.
    • Zomato Pro members must carry valid ID proof at the time of redeeming the dine out discount.
    • Takeaway is not valid on Zomato Pro.
    • The discounts are not valid on special occasions such as New Year’s Eve, Diwali Day, Durga Puja, Christmas Eve, etc.

    Zomato Pro Price

    Zomato Pro benefits and new subscription deal comes at a price that will surely seem like a bargain if you are a regular customer of the Zomato app. You will find that the pricing will make up for the amount of money you will be saving when ordering from Zomato using the Pro feature. There is a Zomato Pro 1 year membership and a 3-month membership currently on offer.

    There is also a new pro subscription that has been offered called the Zomato Pro Plus Membership. With the Pro Plus subscription, one can acquire free delivery on all orders. There is no distance fee and no surge fee even during rush hour. You are also entitled to additional discounts on delivery and dining.
